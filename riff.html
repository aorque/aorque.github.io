How it works

1

One person opens riff.fun

You get a 4-digit code and a shareable link. No signup, no download.

2

The other person joins

Tap the link or enter the code. You're both in the same session instantly.

3

Cards feed you both in real time

Prompts, questions, and dares that escalate as you go. Stupid, then interesting, then real.

4

Session expires. Gone.

Two hours. Timer visible the whole time. When it's done, it's done. No trace, no record.
